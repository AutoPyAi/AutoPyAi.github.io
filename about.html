About Me

I am Suhail Asghar, a software developer with expertise in AI (RAG systems, Agentic AI, Chatbots) and Python automation. My mission is to create smart, user-friendly applications that save time, reduce repetitive work, and boost productivity.

Why I Built This

I noticed how much time professionals waste copying data from YouTube, PDFs, and Excel files manually. So I started building tools that automate these tasks — not with complex cloud APIs, but with simple, offline Python scripts anyone can run.

These tools are designed for people like you: researchers, analysts, small business owners, and students who need results — not complexity.

My Philosophy

Privacy matters. No tracking. No ads. No subscriptions. You own your data — and your tools.

Every tool I build is:

  • ✅ User friendly — easy to use
  • ✅ Runs offline — no internet required after setup
  • ✅ One-time payment — lifetime updates included
